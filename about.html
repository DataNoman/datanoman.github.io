About Me

Hi, I’m Noman Alam, a driven BSc Data Science student at United International University (UIU) with a passion for transforming data into actionable insights. Proficient in Python, statistical analysis, and machine learning, I balance my academic studies with hands-on experience as an Intern and Course Facilitator at Nobelnavigators. Here, I apply data science techniques to real-world problems while helping others learn key concepts—bridging theory with practice.

My expertise includes data cleaning, visualization, and predictive modeling, allowing me to uncover trends and communicate findings effectively. Whether working on projects or mentoring peers, I focus on simplicity and impact, ensuring data-driven solutions are both practical and accessible. My goal is to keep refining my skills in analytics, machine learning, and technical instruction to become a well-rounded data professional.

I’m eager to collaborate on meaningful projects and contribute to the data science community. Let’s connect!
